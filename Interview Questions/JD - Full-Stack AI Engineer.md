## Responsibilities

- Design, develop, and implement scalable AI solutions and intelligent agents from conception to deployment.
- Build, configure, and maintain dedicated servers, including MCP servers, to host and interact with AI applications and agents.
- Develop custom AI agents capable of performing complex tasks, interacting dynamically with environments, and responding intelligently to diverse inputs.
- Integrate and leverage various large language models (LLMs) such as OpenAI, Google Gemini, and others, to enhance agent capabilities and facilitate natural language understanding and generation.
- Write clean, efficient, and well-documented code primarily in Python, JavaScript, and TypeScript.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including product managers, designers, and other developers, to define requirements and deliver high-quality AI features.
- Conduct thorough testing, debugging, and optimization of AI models and applications to ensure performance, reliability, and security.
- Stay up-to-date with the latest advancements in AI, machine learning, and relevant technologies, and apply new knowledge to projects.
- Troubleshoot and resolve technical issues related to AI systems and server infrastructure.
