---

**User Authentication & Security**
- Email / Phone login & signup 
- Two-factor authentication (2FA)

**User & Role Management**
- Admin, Finance Officer, Auditor, Volunteer roles
- Access control (who can view/add/approve)

**Account Management**
- Link multiple bank accounts & cash account (manual entry)
- View balances across accounts

**Donation & Income Tracking**
- Record one-time and recurring donations
- Multi-channel support (online payments, checks, bank transfers, cash)
- Donor profiles with history (Donor Name - Email
- Acknowledgment receipts (auto-generated & emailed)

**Grant & Funding Management** **(Need to discuss)**
- Track grants received (amount, donor, purpose)
- Set conditions/restrictions for funds
- Reporting to grant providers

**Expense Tracking**
- Categorized expenses (programs, admin, fundraising, etc.)
- Upload receipts (image/PDF)
- Approval workflows for expenses
- Reimbursements for staff/volunteers
- Salary Expense

**Budgeting**
- Annual & project-based budgets
- Compare actual vs. planned spending
- Alerts for over-budget expenses

**Financial Reports**
- Income vs. Expense reports
- Fund utilization reports 
- Balance sheets for donors & board 
- Download in PDF/Excel

**Donor Reporting**
- Individual donor contribution summaries 
- Impact reports linked to projects funded

**Audit & Compliance**
- Audit trail of all transactions
- Role-based approvals
